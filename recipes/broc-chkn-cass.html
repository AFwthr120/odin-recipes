Broccoli Chicken Casserole

This is a quick, easy, and inexpensive recipe that everyone is sure to love, even my picky eater loves it!

Ingredients

  • 1- 6 oz. pkg. chicken stuffing mix
  • 2 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 1 cup frozen broccoli florets, thawed (can use fresh broccoli if wanted)
  • 1- 10 3/4 oz. can condensed broccoli cheese soup, undiluted.
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Prepare stuffing mix according to package, using 1 1/2 cups of water
  3. In large bowl, combine chicken, broccoli, and soup
  4. Transfer to greased 11x7-in. baking dish. Top with stuffing and sprinkle with cheese.
  5. Bake, covered, for 20 mins.
  6. Uncover and bake until heated through for 10-15 mins. longer
