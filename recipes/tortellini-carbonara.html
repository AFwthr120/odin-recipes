Tortellini Carbonara

This recipe is shows how to make a quick and easy dinner option that looks and tastes like a dish from a fancy italian restuarant.

Ingredients

  • i 9 oz. pkg. refridgerated cheese tortellini
  • 8 backon strips, chopped (I use precooked bacon)
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cook tortellini according to package directions; drain.
  2. While tortellini cooks, cook bacon in skillet, remove, and drain on paper towel. If using precooked bacon, skip this step.
  3. In large skillet, combine the heavy cream, cheese, parsley, and bacon; heat through over medium heat.
  4. Stir in tortellini; serve immediately and enjoy!
